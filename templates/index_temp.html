About CRAC Group

CRAC (Community Resilience Against Crisis) is a non-profit initiative formed by a number of concerned NGOs, Celebrities and Private Institutions focused on providing quick response and building the resilience for affected communities with the sole aim of contributing selflessly to the wellbeing of the common Nigerian through;

  • Food support drive
  • Aggressive enlightenment initiatives and educational initiatives
  • Altruistic projects & sustainable strategic interventions
  • Ultimately creating a platform for Nigerians by Nigerians for responding to disaster nationwide.

A collaborative response to COVID 19 pandemic by concerned non-profits, individuals, startups, celebrities & private institution by building the resilience (capacities and skill that ordinary people can use for their own benefit and the benefit of their community) to respond to the impact of traumatic and/or stressful experiences linked to COVID 19.

THE PROBLEM

The negative Impact associated with existence of COVID 19 pandemic (trauma, sickness arising from fear and the price hike caused from panic buying) coupled with the control measures, such as the stay at home policy on the populace, will badly affect people especially the most vulnerable who depends on their daily activity to feed and run their lives.

THE SOLUTION

To CRAC, the solution is “Resilience” which is response and management, knowing that the biggest support can come from the crowd and hence, we have created a crowd-product donation platform for people to make donations of food items nationwide.

As we simultaneously support with Social Health Interventions Project covering; Mental Health, Re-Integration Support & Trauma Care Center, with the future in mind.

FAQ

WHO OWNS COMMUNITY RESILIENCE AGAINST CRISIS (CRAC)?

All Founders/coordinator which comprises of the non-profit, celebrities and some private companies project manage CRAC but the people of Nigeria own CRAC.

WHAT ARE THE MAIN OBJECTIVES OF CRAC?

Delivery of social, economic and well-being related relief to Nigerians during and after the scourge of crisis through strategic support and intervention initiatives focused on building the Resilience of Nigerians.

This project will kick start with resilience against COVID 19 through the robust Community structure of Nigeria.

We hope this will help to;

1. Food support drive 2. Aggressive educational initiatives associated to COVID 19 3. Altruistic projects & sustainable strategic interventions 4. Ultimately creating a platform for Nigerians by Nigerians for responding to disaster nationwide
WHAT IS THE PURPOSE OF THIS FOOD DONATION?

The Community Resilience Against Crisis is a resilience initiative that enables individuals, celebrities, corporations, foundations, and other organizations support the vulnerable with food items.

WHERE DO THE FOOD ITEMS GO?

CRAC will receive food donations, which will then be distributed transparently and managed by the coordinators to the most vulnerable.

WHAT PERCENTAGE OF EACH DONATION WILL GO TO BENEFICIARIES?

100% of donations made will go towards the beneficiaries, with 5% of cash donations used to cover the costs of online transactions (Approx. 2%), administrative & logistic cost (Approx. 3%).

HOW IS THIS FOOD DONATIONS DIFFERENT FROM OTHER DONATIONS IN THE COUNTRY?

This donation is structured as a crowd donation system that allows everyone with as low as N100 to participate, giving all an opportunity to respond to the crisis.
“#ThisIsMyResponse #BecauseIcan”

WHAT ARE THE DISTRIBUTION CHANNELS OR METHOD?

Distribution will majorly be through the volunteer network that we are building in every locality (774 LGA and 8,809 Wards) and we expect this to grow.

We will leverage the logistic partners also to help get the food to the volunteers that will do the final round of distribution.

We will keep thinking the distribution system and open to suggestions. Please feel free to send us an email; info@cracgroup.org or call Sam on +234 813 840 7353

CAN I EMBARK FOOD DONATION TO A SPECIFIC STATE?

Yes you can. But you can email; info@cracgroup.org to discuss your specific needs

DOES CRAC SEND FOOD OR MONEY DIRECTLY TO GOVERNMENTS, OTHER NGOS OR BOTH?

CRAC will not send money directly to institutions or anyone, but will send in some cases materials or food to support institutions as part of our distribution network.

HOW DO WE ENSURE FULL TRANSPARENCY?

Transactions reports would be published online on the website for the public to access.

We have looked at creative ways to make sure everything is transparent;

1. Project account was secured by 2 of the NGOs in the group (we are open to adding more) 2.Signatories are from 3 organizations in the group 3. All funds are escrowed and monitored by the partner bank (Sun Trust Bank) 4. Board & coordinator will manage the distribution of food
WHAT HAPPENS TO EXCESS FOOD OR FUND AFTER THE PANDEMIC IS GONE?

Food obviously will not be excess.

But funds left after the pandemic will be used for the Phase 2 of the project.

Secure The Future International Initiative (SDF) - shall champion the support and reintegration services for selected victims in need.

HOW MUCH FOOD DONATIONS ARE YOU HOPING TO RAISE?

CRAC is hoping to raise food and monetary donations to the tune of N5billion ($15m) from this campaign.

1. Card payment on the website (local and international) 2. Bank Payment on the website 3. Short Code Payment (Donate with airtime) 4. Local Bank Transfer or Deposit 5. International Wire Transfer (www.worldremit.com)
